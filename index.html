About the Trio

The Barcelona trio, comprising Lionel Messi, Neymar, and Luis Suarez, was one of the most formidable attacking forces in football history. During their time together at FC Barcelona from 2014 to 2017, they won numerous titles and set records that will be remembered for years to come.

The combination of Messi's incredible dribbling skills and playmaking abilities, Neymar's flair and goal-scoring prowess, and Suarez's clinical finishing turned Barcelona into an unstoppable force on the field.

Achievements

La Liga Titles

During their time together, the Barcelona trio won the La Liga title 3 times consecutively from 2015 to 2017.

UEFA Champions League Title

In the 2014-2015 season, Barcelona clinched the UEFA Champions League title, with the trio playing a crucial role in their success.

Copa del Rey Titles

Barcelona also won the Copa del Rey title twice during this period, in 2015 and 2016.

FIFA Club World Cup Titles

The trio also helped Barcelona win the FIFA Club World Cup title in 2015.
